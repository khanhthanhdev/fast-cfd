PRODUCT BRIEF
HVAC AI Fast Performance Prediction Platform
AI-Powered Indoor Climate Digital Twin for Healthy and Energy-Efficient Buildings

1. Executive Summary
The HVAC AI Fast Performance Prediction Platform is an AI-powered Indoor Climate Digital Twin that enables engineers, designers, and building managers to rapidly evaluate indoor airflow, thermal comfort, and ventilation performance in less than 15 minutes.
Traditional HVAC performance analysis requires complex CFD simulations that can take hours or days, limiting their use during early design and decision-making stages.
This platform replaces slow simulation workflows with an AI surrogate model trained on high-fidelity CFD data, enabling near-instant prediction of:
Temperature distribution
Airflow velocity fields
Thermal comfort metrics (PMV)
Indoor comfort scores
The system combines fast geometry modeling, HVAC configuration, AI inference, and visualization into a single workflow that supports sustainable building design and healthy indoor environments.
In addition to engineering analysis, the platform can generate automated decision reports to support HVAC system optimization and stakeholder communication.
